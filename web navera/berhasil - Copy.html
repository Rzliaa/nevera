About Me

Hai, Saya Everleaf

Navera Student Company (Navera SC) is a student company from SMAN 16 Bandung that focuses on sustainable fashion by innovating eco-print. Our mission is to blend creativity, environmental responsibility, and community empowerment by turning natural leaves and flowers into exclusive textile designs.

Our flagship product, the Everleaf Eco-Print Vest, demonstrates how fashion can be both stylish and eco-conscious. Each vest has a unique imprint, representing our commitment to zero waste, local sourcing, and short supply chains that support UMKM and reduce environmental impact.

Navera SC is not only about fashion but also about sustainability and innovation showing that students can be at the forefront of creating a greener future.

Product Journey

From Leaf to Vest

Perjalanan panjang namun penuh cinta — setiap tahap dikerjakan dengan kesabaran dan keahlian tangan-tangan terampil.

Step 01

Collecting Leaves & Flowers

We gather fallen leaves and flowers from school grounds, home and Bandung city parks. This ensures zero cost raw materials while reducing organic waste.

Step 02

Eco-Print Pounding Technique

Leaves are carefully placed on cotton fabric and gently hammered so their natural pigments are released. This creates authentic imprints, each with unique colors and shapes.

Step 03

Fabric Preparation

The eco-printed fabric is washed, dried, and finished to preserve the natural motifs. Every piece of fabric carries a one-of-a-kind design.

Washed Dried Finishing
Step 04

Local Tailoring Collaboration

We partner with local MSMEs in Bandung to transform the ecoprinted fabric into stylish vests. This supports small businesses and strengthens our short supply chain.

Step 05

Everleaf Vest ✨

The final product is the Everleaf EcoPrint Vest — a sustainable fashion item that combines creativity, environmental responsibility, and community empowerment.

Care Instructions

Rawat Vest, Jaga Cerita

Produk alami punya karakter unik. Ikuti panduan ini agar motif ecoprint bertahan lama.

Yang Bisa Dilakukan

Cuci tangan dengan air dingin menggunakan deterjen lembut. Jangan direndam lebih dari 15 menit.

Jemur terbalik di tempat teduh. Hindari sinar matahari langsung.

Setrika suhu rendah dari bagian dalam kain. Gunakan kain pelindung untuk bagian motif.

Simpan gantung dengan hanger bahu lebar. Letakkan kain tisu di antara lipatan jika dilipat.

Pakai dry leaf (daun kering) dalam lemari sebagai pengharum alami sekaligus penjaga warna.

Yang Harus Dihindari

Jangan gunakan pemutih atau deterjen berbahan keras. Zat kimia menghancurkan pigmen alami secara permanen.

Jangan mesin cuci atau keringkan dengan mesin. Gaya putaran & panas merusak serat dan motif.

Jangan merendam berhari-hari. Pigmen alami larut dalam air, semakin lama direndam semakin pudar.

Jangan setrika panas langsung di motif. Panas berlebih mengoksidasi dan mengubah warna.

Jangan expose terus-menerus ke cahaya kuat saat tidak dipakai lama.

Karakter Unik Produk Alami

Pigmen ecoprint bersifat living color — warna bisa sedikit berubah seiring waktu, itulah keindahannya. Gradasi halus menandakan vest Anda benar-benar dari bahan alami, bukan tekstil sintetis. Ini bukan cacat, tapi signature of nature.
