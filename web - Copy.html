About Me

Hai, Saya Everleaf

Navera Student Company (Navera SC) is a student company from SMAN 16 Bandung that focuses on sustainable fashion by innovating eco-print. Our mission is to blend creativity, environmental responsibility, and community empowerment by turning natural leaves and flowers into exclusive textile designs.

Our flagship product, the Everleaf Eco-Print Vest, demonstrates how fashion can be both stylish and eco-conscious. Each vest has a unique imprint, representing our commitment to zero waste, local sourcing, and short supply chains that support UMKM and reduce environmental impact.

Navera SC is not only about fashion but also about sustainability and innovation — showing that students can be at the forefront of creating a greener future.

Product Journey

From Leaf to Vest

Perjalanan panjang namun penuh cinta — setiap tahap dikerjakan dengan kesabaran dan keahlian tangan-tangan terampil.

Step 01

Collecting Leaves & Flowers

We gather fallen leaves and flowers from school grounds, home and Bandung city parks.

Step 02

Eco-Print Pounding Technique

Leaves are carefully placed on cotton fabric and gently hammered so their natural pigments are released.

Step 03

Fabric Preparation

The eco-printed fabric is washed, dried, and finished to preserve the natural motifs.

Washed Dried Finishing
Step 04

Local Tailoring Collaboration

We partner with local MSMEs in Bandung to transform the ecoprinted fabric into stylish vests.

Step 05

Everleaf Vest ✨

The final product — a sustainable fashion item that combines creativity, environmental responsibility, and community empowerment.

Care Instructions

Rawat Vest, Jaga Cerita

Yang Bisa Dilakukan

Cuci tangan dengan air dingin menggunakan deterjen lembut.

Jemur terbalik di tempat teduh.

Setrika suhu rendah dari bagian dalam kain.

Simpan gantung dengan hanger bahu lebar.

Pakai dry leaf dalam lemari sebagai pengharum alami.

Yang Harus Dihindari

Jangan gunakan pemutih atau deterjen berbahan keras.

Jangan mesin cuci atau keringkan dengan mesin.

Jangan merendam berhari-hari.

Jangan setrika panas langsung di motif.

Jangan expose terus-menerus ke cahaya kuat.

Karakter Unik Produk Alami

Pigmen ecoprint bersifat living color — warna bisa sedikit berubah seiring waktu. Ini bukan cacat, tapi signature of nature.
